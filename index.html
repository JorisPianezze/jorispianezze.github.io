Joris Pianezze

CNRS Research Engineer — Laboratoire d'aérologie, Toulouse

Specialised in the development of numerical models, I work on the porting, improvement and analysis of a REgional Coupled Ocean-Wave-Atmosphere system for fine-scale simulations. In particular, I am involved in the development of the CROCO and Meso-NH community codes.

This website shares tools and documentation with colleagues and with the wider community working with these models.
